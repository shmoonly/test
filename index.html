About Me

Hello there! I'm Shmoon, my full name is Wayne Czar A. Ricaplaza, a passionate student currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology in the University of San Carlos. I'm deeply fascinated by the world of technology and how it is shaping the future of our world.

My journey into IT began with an interest in space and technology. I created Shmooniverse to reflect both my enthusiasm for IT and my love for the cosmos.

Inspirations

  • Space and the Universe: The boundless beauty and mystery of the cosmos inspire me.
  • Technology: The constant evolution of IT motivates me to learn and grow.

When I'm not working on IT projects, I explore new tech trends, learn about different cultures, and experiment with creative designs. My goal is to continuously improve my skills and contribute meaningfully to the IT field.
